Case Study: Predicting Energy Consumption with Bayesian Logistic Regression

Project Overview

This project aimed to predict whether household energy consumption would exceed the average using temperature as the primary predictor. The dataset, sourced from the UCI Machine Learning Repository, includes an extensive amount of data that was continuously gathered 10-minute intervals in Tetouan City in 2017. This data includes records of energy consumption from the city's different distribution networks as well as meteorological variables like temperature, humidity, and airflows.
